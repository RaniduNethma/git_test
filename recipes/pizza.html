Pizza

Pizza is an Italian dish of flattened bread dough covered with tomato sauce, cheese, and other toppings, then baked. It's often served hot.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • olive oil (for the dough)
  • pizza sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese, and toppings like pepperoni
  • mushrooms
  • onions
  • peppers
  • olives
  • and fresh herbs like basil

Steps

  1. Start with a crust
  2. Add a sauce
  3. Add some veggies
  4. Try some fruit on your pizza
  5. Add some protein
  6. Add cheese
  7. Bake your creation in a hot oven (450 F or above)
  8. Whole-wheat Pizza Crust
